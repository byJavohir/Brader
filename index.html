LUCERNE

Lucerne is a city in Switzerland, in the center of the Swiss plateau, the capital of the German-speaking canton of the same name Lucerne. The city is located on the shore of Lake Lucerne, at the foot of Mount Pilatus. Population: 57,890 people (2006)

Attractions

Lucerne is rich in sights and it is worth getting acquainted with on foot, because the Old Town is a small pedestrian zone. Many buildings are decorated with frescoes or paintings telling about the purpose of a particular building or the historical events thanks to which the building was built.

Transport accessibility

Lucerne is connected by rail to all major cities in Switzerland. The nearest international airport is 70 km away in Zurich.
